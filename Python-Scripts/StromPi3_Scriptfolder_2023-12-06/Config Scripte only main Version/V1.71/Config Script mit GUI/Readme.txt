This script only works under Python3 you also need to get the following librarys wich you can download with the command listed below:
sudo apt-get install python3-pil python3-pil.imagetk

The Maximum and Mimimum values are listet here:
Shutdown Timer:			 	0 or higher and 65535 or lower
Button Shutdown Timer:	 	0 or higher and 65535 or lower
Interval On-Time minutes:	0 or higher and 59 or lower
Interval Off-Time minutes:	0 or higher and 59 or lower
Real Time Clock Hours:		0 or higher and 23 or lower
Real Time Clock Minutes:	0 or higher and 59 or lower
Real Time Clock Days:		1 or higher and 31 or lower
Real Time Clock Years:		0 or higher and 99 or lower
Power-Off Day:				1 or higher and 31 or lower
WakeUp-Alarm Minutes:		0 or higher and 59 or lower
Power-Off Minutes:			0 or higher and 59 or lower
WakeUp-Alarm Hours:			0 or higher and 23 or lower
Power-Off Hours:			0 or higher and 23 or lower
